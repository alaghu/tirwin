NAME & OBJECTIVE

VALUES

TIRWIN believes that the way the business is run is more important than what the business can bring in to its shareholders.

TIRWIN commits itself to integrate all its business activities with its faith in-
  •  Integrity
  •  Respect
  •  Ethical Business Practices
  •  Performance Excellence
  •  Commitment to Community Development

B. Govindarajan (Air India Govind) heads TIRWIN as its Chief Operating Officer.

After more than 30 years of fulfilling service but nearly sixty-four months early for his scheduled retirement, he opted for a voluntary retirement from Air India to create and make TIRWIN a Leader in People Business - Sharing Knowledge and Ensuring Expertise in the airline operations.

He brings with him more than thirty-four years of both hands-on and managerial experience in airline operations. He has undertaken various projects - Development of Systems and Procedures, Quality Management, Performance Audits etc., in India and abroad.
Govind - a celebrity trainer is an invited speaker for national and international conferences. He was a member of IATA - DGTTF. He also writes his views on logistics subjects in leading business newspapers and magazines.

TIRWIN TEAM consists of Advisors, Domain Knowledge Experts, Subject Matter Experts, Associates who bring with them wealth of varied experience and credibility in the industry.
